Hello. My name is Ege. I am 17 years old. I was born in 2006 in Ankara. When I was younger, I was very naughty and curious. First, I studied at Ahmet Hamdi Tanpınar Primary School for 4 years, after finishing primary school, I studied at Ahmet Hamdi Tanpınar Secondary School for 2 years. I had to change my school due to my family moving. I studied at Sincan / Gazneliler Secondary School for 1 year, then I studied for 1 year at Yenimahalle / Şehit Çağdaş Tamkoç Secondary School because a new school was built next to our house. Towards the end of secondary school, Covid-19 disease emerged, so I couldn't leave the house very much, at this time I started to deal with software and my choice of high school was thus clear. After I took the LGS exam, I went to Batıkent / Şehit Alper Zor Vocational and Technical Anatolian High School because I was interested in software. I studied Network Administration and Cyber ​​Security. I started my internship in the middle of the 10th grade and started my Cyber ​​Security internship at Kobi Bilişim Güvenlik.
